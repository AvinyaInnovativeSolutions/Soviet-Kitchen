Avil Milk

Origin of the dish

Aval Milk or Avil Milk is a drink sold in the streets of the Malabar region of Kerala, India. It is made with aval which is essentially poha or beaten rice flakes, along with ripe bananas, milk of any kind, and nuts. Avil milk is one of the most popular health drinks sold in Malabar. It is a concoction of mashed bananas, roasted aval or rice flakes, milk and peanuts for an additional crunch.
